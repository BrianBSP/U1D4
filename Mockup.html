Focaccia with Caramelizad Onions,
Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tabelspoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries
